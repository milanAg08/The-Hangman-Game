How to play?

1. Pick a Letter: Click on a letter from the on-screen keyboard to guess the hidden word.

2. Reveal the Word: If the letter is correct, it appears in the word. If not, a part of the hangman is drawn!

3. Limited Guesses: You only have 7 chances to guess wrong. Use them wisely!

4. Win or Lose: Guess the word correctly before the hangman is fully drawn to win. Otherwise, it's game over!

5. Play Again: Finished a round? Hit "Next Word" or "Play Again" to keep the fun going!
